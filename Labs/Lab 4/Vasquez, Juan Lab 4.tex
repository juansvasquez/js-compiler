\section{Exercise 4.4.3}
S -> SS+ | SS* | a

\subsection{$First$ and $Follow$ Sets for Non-terminals}
$First$(S): \\
1 (S S +) = \{+,a,*\} \\
2 (S S *) = \{+,a,*\} \\

Final Answer: \{+,a,*\}
